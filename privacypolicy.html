Privacy Policy

Legal Lens is a free online service that offers a convenient way to process legal documents into key-value organized data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your information.

Information We Collect

When you use the App, we do not collect or store any personal information or legal documents on our servers. We automatically collect certain information about your device solely for the purpose of managing your interaction with our service.

Uploads and Data Storage

We do not store your document uploads or any processed information on our backend. All documents and data are automatically deleted from our servers immediately after processing, and no copies are retained.

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not store your uploads or processed information, we do not use your data for any purpose other than providing you with the processed data during your current session.

Security Measures

We take the security of your information seriously. While we do not store any user data, we ensure that our systems follow adequate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your privacy.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy
